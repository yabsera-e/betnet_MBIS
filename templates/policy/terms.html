BetNet – Digital Business Simulation

Submitted for the Digital Business and Technology Course

1. Introduction

BetNet is an e-commerce simulation platform designed to connect rent seekers with house owners. The platform provides a digital marketplace for property rentals, demonstrating core digital business concepts including listing management, payment simulation, and secure communication.

2. Challenges in the Rental Market

Traditional rental processes face several issues:

  • Information Asymmetry: Rent seekers struggle to access comprehensive and reliable property details.
  • Time Inefficiency: Physical visits and multiple inquiries prolong the rental process.
  • Limited Trust: House owners cannot easily verify serious inquiries, leading to wasted time.
  • Payment Uncertainty: Ensuring secure and verifiable transactions is challenging.

3. BetNet Solution

BetNet addresses these challenges by providing a digital platform where:

  • House owners can post listings with detailed descriptions, photos, rent amount, and location.
  • A small listing fee ensures quality and commitment.
  • Rent seekers can filter listings based on price, location, number of bedrooms, and other preferences.
  • Contact with the house owner is enabled only after completing a payment simulation, demonstrating secure inquiry verification.
  • An integrated messaging system allows verified users to communicate safely with house owners.

4. How It Works

  1. Listing Creation: House owners submit their property details, upload images, and pay a listing fee to make the property visible.
  2. Search and Filtering: Rent seekers explore available listings and apply filters to find properties matching their requirements.
  3. Payment for Contact: Rent seekers simulate a small payment to gain access to house owner contact information and messaging.
  4. Communication: Verified users can send and receive messages securely through the platform’s integrated chat.

5. Benefits

BetNet demonstrates practical digital business processes:

  • Centralized rental marketplace for efficiency and transparency.
  • Simulation of secure payment workflows for verified inquiries.
  • Improved trust between house owners and rent seekers through controlled communication.
  • Hands-on exposure to e-commerce principles for students.
